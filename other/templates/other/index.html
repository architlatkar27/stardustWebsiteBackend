About Us

Team S.T.A.R.D.U.ST was founded in 2019 under the support and guidance of the Department of Electronics and Communication engineering by a group of 3rd year UG students at Ramaiah Institute of Technology with a clear objective of designing and building a 2U Cubesat having a novel payload idea and launching it with the support and guidance of college Faculty. A new but important addition to this team in the year 2021 is a Rocketry Division with the sole aim to design and fabricate a sounding rocket. Other objectives of this club include representing the college in space conferences, competitions, symposiums and other national and global events on Space science and technology.

Mission


Enhance the skills of undergraduate engineering students by providing hands-on experience for building a small Space

Vision

To successfully design, fabricate and operate an indeginous small Space with the purpose of providing useful contribution in the field of Space Technology
