S.T.A.R.D.U.ST comprises of 6 main subsytems

Subsystems

Attitude Determination and Control System



ADCS
very essential for stabilising the satellite in the orbit and ensuring that it points in the direction it is supposed to point.

Electrical and Power System



EPS
it is used for efficient and reliable power generation,its conversion to differrent power and voltage levels, power storage and proper distribution to various subsystems on-board satellite according to their requirements

Ground Control And Communication System



G&C
the comunications subsytems which let us know if our satellite is as good as a rock in space. It is responsible for ensuring telecommunication between the satellite and ground station.

OnBoard Command and Data Handling System.



ODHS
The Onboard data handling subsytem is essentially the "brain" of the orbitor and controls all spacecraft functions. it carries and stored data between the various electronic units and the ground segment, via the tekemetry, tracking and command(TT&C) susbsytem.

PAYLOAD



PAYLOAD
It refers to the actual function of the satellite. In other words, its the purpose of the satellite. Most satellite teams have a payload subsytem that works on deciding the purpose and the methodology of achieving this aim.

Structures and Thermal System



S&T
It is responsible for designing and manufacturing the satellite structure while ensuring that the satellite can bear all the structural and the thermal loads. It also maintains the temperature of all spacecraft components within certain limits.
